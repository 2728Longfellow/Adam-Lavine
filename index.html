About Adam

Adam Noah Levine is an American singer, songwriter, and musician, who is the lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist for the pop rock band Maroon 5. Levine began his musical career in 1994 with the band Kara's Flowers, of which he was the lead vocalist and lead guitarist.

With Maroon 5, he has released numerous multi-platinum albums and chart-topping singles, including "This Love," "Moves Like Jagger," and "Girls Like You." Known for his distinctive tenor voice, Levine has also served as a coach on NBC's reality talent show The Voice from 2011 to 2019.

Beyond music, he has ventured into acting and business, establishing himself as a versatile and influential figure in the entertainment industry.
